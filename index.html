TRIANGLE-TRACKER

The Triangle-Tracker is an application that will help users know whether a triangle is Isosceles, Equilateral,Scalene or NOT a triangle at all.

The equilateral.

In the equilateral triangle, all the sides are the same length
and all the angles are the same size.

The isosceles

The isosceles triangle has two sides that are the same length
and two angles that are the same size.

The scalene

The scalene triangle has no side equal
and all the angles are different.
